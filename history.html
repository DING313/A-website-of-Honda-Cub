The best-sold motorcycle among world

The first Honda cub was created by Soichiro Honda and his group, As a brilliant car engineer, and the founder of Honda, his first creation immediately become a miracle shining in automobiles history. It was the Super Cub.

The Prime Generations

As the eldest member of cub family,the Super Cub C100 was a significant invation, for the reason of its elegant shape, durable engine and cost-effective. the classic appearance of cub family is still taken in Nowadays design

1980s-2000s

During these years, the cub family was getting stronger. Developed from Super Cub, there were Hunter Cub series for off-road adventure, Press Cub for delivering foods. At that time the cub series also quite popular in Europe and North American.

Nowadays Cubs

In today's market, we could find quite a lot motorcycles with stronger power and larger size, however the cub family still energetic. Youngsters prefer its delicate and interesting. It gradually become a culture, and a symbol of relaxing, free lifestyle
