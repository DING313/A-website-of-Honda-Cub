Vintage Style

As a vast lineup with over 50 years of history, the Honda cub series exudes a retro charm that is highly sought after by many fashion enthusiasts.

High Modification Potential

The Cub features a highly developed modification system. You can find aftermarket parts available in almost any country worldwide, allowing you to assemble a custom style of your own.

Regular of Animes

More than half a century, Honda Cub series have gradually integrated into the daily lives of many families, becoming a cultural symbol and appearing in numerous Japanese anime, such as "Weathering with You" and "Super Cub and Me."

Kick Start

To this day, even the latest models of Honda Cub retain the traditional kick-start mechanism, which imparts a sense of mechanical authenticity that sets them apart from other modern vehicles.

Unique Clutch

Unlike most motorcycles, the Honda Cub's unique clutch design allows riders to operate without manually controlling the clutch. Additionally, its four-speed gear system enhances the flexibility and enjoyment of riding .
